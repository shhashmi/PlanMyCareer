# Privacy Policy for PlanMyCareer

**Last Updated: January 7, 2026**

## Introduction

PlanMyCareer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our career assessment application and authenticate using Google Sign-In.

## Information We Collect

### Information from Google OAuth

When you sign in to PlanMyCareer using Google OAuth, we collect the following information from your Google account:

- **Email address**: Used to create and identify your account
- **Full name**: Used to personalize your experience
- **Profile picture**: Displayed in your user profile (optional)
- **Google User ID**: Used to link your Google account to your PlanMyCareer account

### Information You Provide

- **Assessment responses**: Your answers to career assessment questions
- **Skills data**: Information about your professional skills and competencies
- **Career preferences**: Information you provide about your career goals and interests

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage data**: Information about how you interact with our application
- **Device information**: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
- **Log data**: IP address, access times, and pages viewed

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and maintain our service**: Create your account, authenticate your identity, and deliver career assessment functionality
2. **Personalize your experience**: Display your name and profile information, customize content based on your preferences
3. **Improve our service**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality and user experience
4. **Communicate with you**: Send important updates about your account or our service
5. **Security and fraud prevention**: Protect against unauthorized access and ensure platform security

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

- **Consent**: You provide explicit consent when you authorize Google OAuth access
- **Contract performance**: Processing is necessary to provide the services you requested
- **Legitimate interests**: We have a legitimate interest in improving our service and preventing fraud

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **With your consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing
- **Service providers**: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our application (e.g., AWS for hosting), under strict confidentiality agreements
- **Legal requirements**: When required by law, court order, or legal process
- **Business transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage

- Your data is stored on secure servers hosted on AWS App Runner
- Google OAuth tokens are handled securely and never stored in plain text
- JWT tokens are used for session management with appropriate expiration times

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure token-based authentication (JWT)
- CSRF protection using state parameters in OAuth flow
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication requirements

Despite our security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
